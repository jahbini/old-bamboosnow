What exactly is Golden Bamboo Snow?

Because of Saipan''s very intense sunlight, clean air, frequent refreshing rains all natural processes on Saipan are greatly sped up.  That incluces growth of all sorts.  One is the production of Bamboo Snow: a natural result of Saipan''s unique ecology with our specific species of Bamboo.

The bamboo culms actually will erupt in miniature volcanos of bamboo snow for several months after harvest.  We carefully collect the snow from the bamboo stalks, and grade each batch according to it''s merits.

While not very good for construction material, the snow has unique properties.  Production needs special care to insure the highly desired properties are enhanced.  BambooCanDo is perhaps the only source on hte expertise necessary for high quality continuous production of Golden Bamboo Snow.
