Snow appears directly on the surface!

Bamboo Snow is a discovery of monumental implications: it is a new Honey, a new Silk. An insect produced commodity, with practical, sustainable uses that stagger the imagination. It even makes your drains run cleaner.

Unlike honey or silk, this totally natural blessing has lain undiscovered at our feet for 10,000 years.  Until now.

Bamboo Snow is a water optional, cleaner and polisher for hard surfaces, glass, plastic, hands, skin, hair, fur, brushes and rugs. It is the mechanic's wife's best friend.

Bamboo Snow can be produced in many areas of the world, but Golden Bamboo Snow from the tropic island of Saipan has superior characteristics.

Simply put, Golden Bamboo Snow agressively absorbs oil and water. It will remove oil, grease, gum or glue from hair or fur in moments.  It will absorb the sweat and oil from your hands leaving them dry and smooth.

Bamboo Snow is so dry that it will turn household waste into garbage jerky and no bacteria can grow, no insect, or vermin will want it.  Add water, though, and it will quickly turn into compost for your garden.  It controls bacteria in a natural way, so it won't produce high-tech hybred superbugs.

It ALSO makes a great additive for construction and creates fire suppressing material for panels and fixtures. Bamboo Snow can suck up 3 times it's weight in glue, and another 2 times it's weight in water. It turn it all into putty which can be molded, extruded, shaped and stuffed into cracks.  And then it will clean up the mess in moments.

Bamboo Snow will absorb nearly any liquid, and will dehydrate anything you put in it.  Keep it dry, and it will provide dry sanitation, keeping harmful bacteria OUT of the water supply.  This would be useful anywhere that water is in short supply.

Bamboo Snow can be produced in South America, Asia, Africa and Oceania. Anyone who has lived in a bamboo structure in the last 10,000 years has been exposed to bamboo snow.  Humans are so tolerant of bamboo snow that it has escaped our attention for all that time.

And if Bamboo Snow really can help water conservation and human sanitation around the world, that would be an enhancement to civilization, wouldn't it?

So help the advancement of civilization by buying and using Golden Bamboo Snow.  Become a friend of bamboo snow by joining the Modern Marianas Society.

If you want to produce Bamboo Snow, you can join the Modern Marianas Society or, if you live on one of the islands polynesia, micronesia or melanesia you can join The Bamboo Can Do cooperative.
